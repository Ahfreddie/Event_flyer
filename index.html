Stockholm Starlight Fair

A magical evening where the city lights meet the stars and fairground thrills

Date: September 12, 2026

Place: Djurgården Island, Stockholm

Highlights:

  • Rooftop stargazing with telescopes over the water
  • Classic Swedish carnival rides with a neon twist
  • Live indie-pop & electronic fusion bands
  • Midnight light parade with lanterns reflecting in the water

Special Guests:

  • Astrid Norén (Swedish indie-pop singer)
  • The Aurora Dancers (glow-in-the-dark performance troupe)
  • Chef Björn & Team Nordic (Scandi fusion tasting menu)

Stalls:

  • Fika & Flicker - coffee, pastries, and miniature lanterns
  • VattenVibe - cocktails & sparkling beverages by the canal
  • Nordic Knots - handmade jewelry and crafts
  • Stockholm Sweetscape - candy, chocolates, and marshmallows
